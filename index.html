English Proficiency Levels

A1 Beginner

What you'll learn:

  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Simple everyday expressions
  • Basic personal information
  • Simple sentences about familiar topics

A2 Elementary

What you'll learn:

  • Frequently used expressions
  • Routine tasks and descriptions
  • Simple direct communication
  • Basic personal and family information

B1 Intermediate

What you'll learn:

  • Main points of clear standard input
  • Deal with most situations while traveling
  • Describe experiences and events
  • Dreams, hopes and ambitions

B2 Upper Intermediate

What you'll learn:

  • Main ideas of complex text
  • Interact with fluency and spontaneity
  • Detailed text on wide range of subjects
  • Advantages and disadvantages of viewpoints

C1 Advanced

What you'll learn:

  • Longer and demanding texts
  • Express ideas fluently and spontaneously
  • Use language flexibly and effectively
  • Clear, well-structured detailed text

C2 Proficiency

What you'll learn:

  • Everything heard or read with ease
  • Summarize information from different sources
  • Express yourself spontaneously and fluently
  • Differentiate finer shades of meaning
